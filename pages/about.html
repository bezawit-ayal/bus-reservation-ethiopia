About EthioBus

Your trusted partner for bus travel across Ethiopia

Our Story

EthioBus was founded with a simple mission: to make bus travel across Ethiopia easier, safer, and more convenient. We understand the importance of reliable transportation in connecting families, businesses, and communities across our beautiful country.

Starting from Addis Ababa, we have expanded our network to cover all major cities including Bahir Dar, Gondar, Hawassa, Mekelle, Dire Dawa, Jimma, and many more. Our platform connects passengers with trusted bus operators, offering a seamless booking experience from the comfort of their homes.

Today, EthioBus serves thousands of travelers every month, helping them reach their destinations safely and on time.

Our Mission

To provide a reliable, user-friendly online bus reservation platform that connects travelers with quality bus services across Ethiopia, making transportation accessible to everyone.

Our Vision

To become Ethiopia's leading digital transportation platform, revolutionizing how people travel by road while promoting safe and comfortable journeys.
