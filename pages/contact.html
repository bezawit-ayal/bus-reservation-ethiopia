Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How do I book a bus ticket?

Simply search for your route, select your preferred bus and seats, fill in passenger details, and confirm your booking. You'll receive an e-ticket via email.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking up to 24 hours before departure. Please contact our support team for cancellation requests.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept cash payments at our stations. Online payment options including Telebirr and CBE Birr are coming soon.

How early should I arrive at the station?

We recommend arriving at least 30 minutes before the scheduled departure time to complete check-in.
